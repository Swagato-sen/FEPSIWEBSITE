Riviera Events


Happiness is meant to be shared...


Riviera is the annual sporting and cultural carnival of the Vellore Institute of Technology. It is an international four-day event consisting of sports competitions, social and cultural events, and star-studded concerts. During Riviera, FEPSI hosts Agnee- an event recognized by the Limca Book of Records, and Happiness Stall- a charity sale to help the needy.

Agnee

FEPSI organized Agnee to pay homage to our beloved former President, Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, affectionately known as the "Missile Man of India". Known for his pivotal role in developing the ‘AGNI’ missile, Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam is one of the finest scientists that the world has witnessed. Agnee aimed to break the record for the largest gathering of people at one place in the formation of a missile. As a result of combined efforts, we achieved validation from the Limca Book of Records. Agnee constituted a total participation count of 1,489 and was witnessed by the Vice President of VIT, Mr. G.V. Selvam, along with eight high-ranking government officials, on 11th February 2018 as a pre-Riviera’18 event.

Happiness Stall

Fepsi's Happy Hive was a stall put up for Riviera 2020, in an effort to raise funds for orphanages in and around Vellore.

Through the ingenuity and hard work of our members, we created decorations and trinkets including bookmarks, tiaras, dream catchers and roses. These were sold alongside dupattas, earrings, and bangles to support the handicrafts industry. Everyone who visited the stall left in smiles, as were those who ran it throughout Riviera.

The profit we collected was the cherry on top, allowing us to help the people over at Sacred Hearts, while creating memories we will never forget!
